Fat and Happy I don’t know about any of you, but food does not always make me happy. There are times when certain foods sound good at the moment and taking that first bite helps with my craving. I take a second bite and it quiet wasn’t the same as the first and each bite after that the craving continues to decline until you finish the meal and feel unsatisfied. I become unhappy and fat. There were no perks. I’ll create the solution, become fat and happy. These recipes are known to be comfort food and they may not be the healthiest, but I know you’ll be happy afterwards.
