Fat and Happy I don’t know about any of you, but food does not always make me happy. There are times when certain foods sound good at the moment and taking that first bite helps with my craving. I take a second bite and it quiet wasn’t the same the first and each bite after that the craving continues to decline until you finish the meal and feel gross. I become unhappy and fat. There’s no perks. I’ll create the solution, become fat and happy. These recipes are known to be comfort food and they may not be the healthiest, but I know you’ll be happy afterwards.

Why will people come to your site? People will come to my site to get recipes that will feed them and their endorphins. These foods will fill you up with joy and happiness. The recipes are simple and user friendly to follow without the extra content. I don’t like when other sites have articles about the recipe, I just want the recipe without all the extras. I believe that’s what the people want. The content without the additional information. What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? What foods will not only feed them but make them feel good about eating the foods. They will have to put a little effort into their food but it’s only a little. If you’ve never cooked before this site will be easy to navigate the recipes easy to follow. What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? They will want to find recipes What information is necessary to complete any actions your sites offer? Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions? Visitors will come to my site rather than the other ones because the simplicity. Google is known for their simple search page; I want my site to have the simplicity and success. I want to create a site that gets ride of the extra distracting ads and graphics and put what the people want and only what the people want. I don’t want to go onto a site to click through a maze just to find a simple recipe. The fewer the clicks the happier you’ll be and that’s what I hope to create with fat and happy.
